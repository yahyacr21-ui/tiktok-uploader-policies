Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Makeshortseasy handles your information when you use our services to upload videos to TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

Our tool is designed to process data locally on your machine. We do not operate a central database or store your personal content on our servers.

  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: Tokens are stored locally on your device.
  • Video Files: The tool processes local files only for uploading purposes.

2. Data Security

Since data is stored locally, the security of your tokens depends on the security of your local environment.
