Privacy Policy for Makeshortseasy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Makeshortseasy ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our desktop application and TikTok integration features. This page belongs to Makeshortseasy and explains what information the application may access, how that information is used, how it is stored on your device, and the choices available to you.

1. Introduction

Makeshortseasy is a desktop application designed to help users connect their TikTok accounts through TikTok’s official authorization flow and upload videos to their own TikTok accounts using TikTok API services. The website associated with Makeshortseasy is used as the official product website and to host legal information. The core app experience takes place on the user’s own device.

Makeshortseasy does not ask you to provide your TikTok password directly. Instead, when account access is needed, the app uses TikTok’s official authorization process.

2. Information Makeshortseasy May Access

Makeshortseasy only accesses information that is necessary for the features you choose to use and that you explicitly authorize through TikTok’s official flow or through your direct use of the app.

  • Basic TikTok account or profile information made available through the authorized TikTok integration, as needed for authentication and connected account functionality.
  • OAuth authorization data, such as authorization codes, access tokens, and refresh tokens, issued through TikTok’s official authorization flow.
  • Videos, captions, and related posting details that you choose from your own device and explicitly submit for upload.
  • Local application logs or error details created on your device for troubleshooting and operational purposes.

3. TikTok Data and Authorization

Makeshortseasy uses TikTok Login Kit for Desktop and TikTok Content Posting API to help you connect your TikTok account and upload content that you choose. The app only uses TikTok-related data and permissions that are required for these features and that you authorize.

When you connect your TikTok account, TikTok may provide Makeshortseasy with authorization data and limited account information permitted by the scopes you approve. Makeshortseasy uses that information only to support the authorized TikTok connection and the user-initiated upload workflow.

4. Local Storage of Tokens

Access tokens and refresh tokens used by Makeshortseasy are stored locally on your own device so the desktop application can maintain an authorized connection between sessions. Makeshortseasy is not described here as storing those tokens on our remote servers.

Because the app runs locally, the protection of the device itself also matters. Anyone with access to your device and local application files may be able to access locally stored app data, depending on your device configuration and security settings.

5. User-Selected Media and Captions

Makeshortseasy may process videos, captions, and related posting inputs that you select from your own device. This information is used only to prepare and submit the upload you explicitly initiate to your own TikTok account.

Makeshortseasy does not upload content automatically without user action. Uploads occur only when you instruct the app to do so.

6. Local Logs and Error Information

Makeshortseasy may create local logs, status records, or error messages on your device to help with troubleshooting, diagnostics, or understanding whether a TikTok connection or upload action succeeded or failed. These logs are generated locally as part of the desktop application experience.

Unless you choose to share local logs yourself, this Privacy Policy does not represent that such logs are automatically transmitted to a remote backend.

7. How Makeshortseasy Uses Information

Makeshortseasy uses authorized and locally available information to:

  • authenticate your TikTok connection through TikTok’s official flow;
  • maintain the authorized session on your device using locally stored tokens;
  • allow you to select media and submit uploads to your own TikTok account;
  • display connection, posting, and error status within the desktop application; and
  • support troubleshooting and technical operation through local logs or error records.

8. Data Retention

Makeshortseasy retains locally stored tokens, local logs, and other local app data on your device for as long as they remain on that device, are needed for the app’s functionality, or until you remove them. User-selected media remains subject to your own device storage and file management practices unless and until you delete it.

If you disconnect the app, revoke access through TikTok, or delete local Makeshortseasy data from your device, the app may no longer be able to use previously stored tokens or related local data.

9. Revoking Access and Deleting Local Data

You can revoke Makeshortseasy’s access to your TikTok account through your TikTok account settings, subject to TikTok’s own controls and interfaces.

You can also delete locally stored tokens, local logs, or other application data from your own device by removing the relevant Makeshortseasy files or reinstalling or resetting the application, depending on how you use it.

If you no longer want Makeshortseasy to access your TikTok account, you should revoke access in TikTok and remove local app data from your device.

10. Security

Makeshortseasy uses a local desktop workflow and TikTok’s official authorization flow, and we aim to handle authorized information in a reasonable and careful manner consistent with the app’s purpose. However, no method of storage, software operation, or device security can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You are responsible for maintaining the security of the device where Makeshortseasy is installed, including account access controls, file access controls, and general system security practices.

11. Children and Eligibility

Makeshortseasy is intended for users who are legally permitted to use TikTok’s services and the relevant TikTok developer features. The app is not directed to children. If you are not old enough to lawfully use the relevant TikTok services or to authorize this type of account connection, you should not use Makeshortseasy.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in Makeshortseasy, its TikTok integration, or related operational practices. When we make changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date shown on this page.
