Privacy Policy for Makeshortseasy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Makeshortseasy ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our desktop application and its integrated TikTok features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our software interacts with your data.

1. Overview of Service

Makeshortseasy is a desktop-based application designed to facilitate the preparation and uploading of video content to TikTok. To provide these services, the app utilizes the official TikTok Login Kit for Desktop and the TikTok Content Posting API.

2. Information Accessed and Stored

Because Makeshortseasy is a local desktop application, we do not operate a remote backend server to store your personal content. The following data is handled:

  • TikTok Account Information: Through the TikTok authorization flow, the app accesses basic profile information (such as your username and avatar) to identify the connected account.
  • Authentication Tokens: Upon successful login, TikTok provides OAuth authorization codes, access tokens, and refresh tokens. These tokens are stored locally on your own device to keep you logged in and enable video uploads.
  • User-Selected Media: The app only accesses video files and captions that you explicitly select from your local storage for the purpose of uploading to TikTok.
  • Local Logs: For troubleshooting and performance monitoring, the app may generate local error logs on your device. These logs remain on your machine and are not automatically transmitted to us.

3. How Information is Used

The information accessed by Makeshortseasy is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity via TikTok’s official authorization flow.
  • To facilitate the transfer of your selected media and captions to your TikTok account.
  • To manage your session and ensure the app remains connected to the TikTok API as authorized.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not store your TikTok data on any remote servers. Your data retention is controlled entirely by you:

  • Local Deletion: You may delete the application's local database and cache at any time to remove all stored tokens and session data from your machine.
  • Revoking Access: You can revoke Makeshortseasy’s access to your TikTok account at any time through the "Security and Login" settings within your TikTok mobile app or website.

5. Third-Party Services

Makeshortseasy interacts with TikTok via official API Services. By using this application, you are also bound by TikTok's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review how TikTok handles data at their official privacy portal.

6. Security

We prioritize the security of your local data. Makeshortseasy uses standard local storage practices to protect your authentication tokens. However, the security of your device is your responsibility; we recommend using OS-level encryption and passwords to secure your local files.

7. Children's Privacy

Makeshortseasy does not knowingly collect or solicit information from children. Use of the app is subject to the age requirements set by TikTok for account ownership.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
