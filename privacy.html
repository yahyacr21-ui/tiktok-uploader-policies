Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Makeshortseasy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle TikTok user data via the TikTok API.

2. Data Collection & Use

We only access data explicitly authorized by you (video upload and basic profile info). All authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store user content. You can revoke access at any time via your TikTok settings. Upon revocation, all local tokens are deleted.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We respect your rights to access, port, and delete your data. For inquiries, contact jaungauos@gmail.com.
