Privacy Policy for Makeshortseasy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Makeshortseasy ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our desktop application and its TikTok integration features. Makeshortseasy is a desktop application that enables you to connect your TikTok account and upload video content directly from your own device.

We built Makeshortseasy with a straightforward principle: your data stays on your device. We do not operate a remote backend that stores your personal information, TikTok credentials, or media files. Please read this policy carefully to understand what information is involved and how it is handled.

Makeshortseasy does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server we control. All authentication tokens and app data are stored locally on your own machine.

Information We Access

When you use Makeshortseasy, the following categories of information may be involved:

  • TikTok account information: Basic profile data (such as your display name and account identifiers) returned by TikTok's API after you authorize the application. This is used solely to confirm the connected account and display it within the app.
  • OAuth authorization codes and tokens: As part of the TikTok Login Kit authorization flow, Makeshortseasy receives a short-lived authorization code, which is exchanged for an access token and refresh token. These tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to servers we operate.
  • User-selected media and captions: When you initiate an upload, Makeshortseasy reads the video file(s) and caption text you have chosen from your own device. This content is sent directly to TikTok's API on your behalf and is not stored or processed by us.
  • Local logs: The app may generate log files on your device for diagnostic and troubleshooting purposes. These logs remain on your machine and are not transmitted to us automatically.

TikTok API & Authorization Scopes

Makeshortseasy uses TikTok's official API services — specifically TikTok Login Kit for Desktop and the TikTok Content Posting API. The application requests only the permissions (scopes) necessary for its core functionality: authenticating your account and submitting video content on your behalf when you explicitly trigger an upload.

Makeshortseasy does not request access to your TikTok password. Authorization is handled entirely through TikTok's official OAuth flow, which directs you to TikTok's own login interface. The OAuth callback is handled locally on your machine.

Your use of TikTok's services through Makeshortseasy is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Local Storage of Tokens

Access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok are stored locally in your application data directory. This storage exists so that you do not need to re-authorize the application on every use. The tokens are not shared with us and are not uploaded to any third-party service by Makeshortseasy.

You are responsible for the security of your own device. We recommend keeping your operating system and security software up to date to protect locally stored data.

How Information Is Used

Makeshortseasy uses the information described above for the following purposes only:

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your authorized session.
  • To display basic account details within the application interface.
  • To submit video files and captions to TikTok's API when you explicitly initiate an upload.
  • To generate local diagnostic logs that help you or our support team troubleshoot issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the core features of Makeshortseasy.

Data Retention

Because Makeshortseasy does not transmit your data to our servers, there is no server-side retention period to describe. Locally stored tokens, logs, and app preferences persist on your device until you choose to delete them or uninstall the application.

TikTok access tokens expire according to TikTok's own token lifecycle policies. Refresh tokens allow the app to renew access without requiring re-authorization, until either the token expires or you revoke access.

Revoking Access & Deleting Local Data

You have full control over the data held by Makeshortseasy:

  • Revoke TikTok authorization: You can disconnect Makeshortseasy from your TikTok account at any time by visiting your TikTok account settings under Security & Permissions → Manage App Permissions and removing Makeshortseasy's access.
  • Delete local data: You can delete locally stored tokens, logs, and preferences by removing the application's data folder from your device, or by uninstalling Makeshortseasy entirely through your operating system's standard uninstall process.

Security

We design Makeshortseasy to minimize the data it handles. Because all sensitive data — including authentication tokens — is stored locally on your own device and never transmitted to servers we control, our security surface is limited.

We make reasonable efforts to follow secure coding practices, but we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data stored on your device. Protecting local files is ultimately your responsibility as the device owner.

Eligibility & Children

Makeshortseasy is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, or under the minimum age required by TikTok in your region. By using the application, you represent that you meet TikTok's eligibility requirements for account creation and API usage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application or applicable requirements. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Makeshortseasy after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
