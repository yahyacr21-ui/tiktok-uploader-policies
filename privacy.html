Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Makeshortseasy ("the Tool") handles your information when you use our services to upload videos to TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

Our tool is designed to process data locally on your machine. We do not operate a central database or store your personal content on our servers.

  • TikTok OAuth Tokens: To upload videos, the tool requests access via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0. These tokens (access and refresh tokens) are stored locally on your device.
  • Video Files: The tool processes video files selected by you from your local storage for the purpose of uploading/posting.

2. How We Use Data

The collected data is used exclusively to facilitate the video upload process to your TikTok account as requested through the tool's interface.

3. Third-Party Services

By using this tool, you are interacting with TikTok's API. We recommend reviewing TikTok's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data on their platform.

4. Data Security

Since data is stored locally, the security of your tokens depends on the security of your local environment.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, you can contact us via our official repository or blog.
